Movement

Textbook Definition

Movement is about controlling the eye's flow through the page. Lines are a good way to accomplish this (whether this is achieved through literal lines, or leading lines in images).

My Definition

Movement is about controlling the viewer's eyes, so they see the design in intended manner, and focus on the intended elements. Leading lines are incredibly powerful, and should be a major consideration of your design.

In summary

Movement is controlling where the reader's eye does.
