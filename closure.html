Closure

Textbook Definition

We fill in gaps to form a perceive shape.

My definition

If something is a common shape or item, we imagine the rest of the image if it isn't visible. For instance, if a cookbook as a zoomed in image of a loaf of bread, we imagine the bread that's "off screen."

In summary

Closure is filling in a shape when it's not 100% there.
