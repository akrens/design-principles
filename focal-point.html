Focal Point

Textbook Definition

The Focal Point is the center of attention in the design or layout.

This can be achieved by white space around the focal point, leading lines, its size, color, are a variety of other methods.

My definition

A focal point is the first thing you really see when looking at a layout.

Making the focal point big, intensely colored, and picking a point according to the rule of thirds are easy ways to ensure your focal point pops!

In summary

Focal point is where your eye goes first.
