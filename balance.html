Balance

Textbook Definition

Balance is about creating designs with "equal weight".

Balance can be radial, symmetrical, or asymmetrical. Radial balance requires that a design can be cut in half in any direction while maintaining symmetry, and is traditionally used for spiritual meanings. Symmetrical balance, also known as formal balance, requires that a design can be cut in half in one direction and be roughly equal on both sides, and is a fairly traditional design style. Asymmetrical balance means the design is not symmetrical at all, however if one were to cut a line at a particular point in the design, the visual weight of the design would be equal on the two sides

My definition

Balance is all about making your design feel ordered. Too much stuff in any one space makes things feel chaotic and claustrophobic.

In summary

Balance is ensuring harmony in your design.
