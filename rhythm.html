Rhythm

Textbook Definition

Rhythm is a pattern, regular or irregular. Repetion of a particular image or color is an easy way to accomplish this.

My definition

Rhythm is a way to maintain a harmonious feeling in a design, without making things boring.

In summary

Rhythm is a design having variations that isn't chaotic.
