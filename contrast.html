Contrast

Textbook Definition

Contrast is juxtaposing unlike things in a design.

For example, consider contrasting small and large design elements, or using contrasting colors.

My definition

Contrast is pairing dissimilar design elements.

This ensures your design is visually interesting, and prevents you from creating designs that are quickly scanned over, or creating walls of text.

In summary

Contrast is elements' being juxtaposed.
